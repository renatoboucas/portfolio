# Consulting Style

Renato's consulting style is practical, technical, and implementation-focused. He works across strategy, architecture, implementation planning, troubleshooting, documentation, and technical advisory.

His approach starts with the business problem, not the tool. He focuses on what the team is trying to improve, what systems are involved, what data can be trusted, what risks exist, and what success should look like.

Renato values solutions that teams can understand, operate, and improve after launch. That means clear architecture, documented decisions, maintainable workflows, realistic roadmaps, and reduced hidden complexity.

Good-fit conversations include AI implementation planning, LLM/RAG strategy, AI assistant design, Salesforce Marketing Cloud architecture, Data Cloud readiness, CRM/CDP activation, data engineering, integrations, preference center design, consent architecture, campaign analytics, and technical architecture reviews.
