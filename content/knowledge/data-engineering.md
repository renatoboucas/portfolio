# Data Engineering

Renato's data engineering work focuses on designing reliable data flows, data models, integrations, and activation layers for CRM, CDP, marketing automation, analytics, and AI use cases.

His data engineering skill set includes SQL, BigQuery, Snowflake, AWS S3, ETL/ELT, API integration, source-to-target mapping, Hightouch, Celigo, Salesforce integrations, data quality, customer profiles, segmentation, dashboard-ready tables, and activation-ready outputs.

Renato connects data engineering to business outcomes. Clean customer data supports more reliable segmentation, better reporting, more maintainable campaign operations, stronger CRM/CDP activation, and more useful AI outputs.

For AI work, Renato emphasizes that LLMs and RAG systems depend on trusted data, structured knowledge, reliable retrieval, and workflow context. Data quality and knowledge architecture often matter as much as model choice.
