# Services

Renato's services are organized around practical AI, data, Salesforce, CRM/CDP, and marketing technology execution.

## AI Implementation Consulting

Renato helps teams move from AI ideas to practical workflows using LLMs, automation, data, and platform integration. This includes AI use-case discovery, roadmap planning, vendor and tool evaluation, workflow automation, human-in-the-loop design, and AI governance basics.

## LLM and RAG Application Strategy

Renato helps teams design AI systems that use retrieval-augmented generation to answer questions from trusted business knowledge, documents, support content, and operational data. This includes knowledge source inventory, RAG architecture, retrieval strategy, prompt framework, and evaluation planning.

## AI Agents and Workflow Automation

Renato helps plan AI-enabled workflows, internal assistants, marketing operations copilots, support assistants, API-connected workflows, and human review checkpoints.

## Salesforce Marketing Cloud Architecture

Renato supports Salesforce Marketing Cloud architecture across Journey Builder, Automation Studio, Contact Builder, Data Extensions, SQL, AMPscript, SSJS, CloudPages, sender profiles, publication lists, and campaign operations.

## Salesforce Data Cloud and CRM/CDP Activation

Renato helps teams prepare customer data for segmentation, identity resolution, consent alignment, Data Cloud readiness, CRM/CDP activation, customer profile strategy, and activation planning.

## Data Engineering and Integration Architecture

Renato designs data pipelines, data models, source-to-target mapping, integration patterns, data quality checks, API integrations, BigQuery, Snowflake, AWS S3, Hightouch, Celigo, Salesforce integrations, and activation-ready outputs.

## Preference Center and Consent Architecture

Renato designs preference centers, publication list behavior, unsubscribe workflows, subscriber status logic, audit logging, consent data models, and governance-minded implementation patterns.

## Campaign Analytics and Reporting Automation

Renato creates reporting-ready data structures and automations for campaign performance, form submissions, UTM tracking, KPI design, dashboard-ready tables, segmentation reporting, and operational analytics.
